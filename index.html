Our Mission

"Feel the love" with Safe Space!

Safe Space is all about creating an environment that encourages self-love and self-motivation.

We want you to feel loved and motivated throughout the day.

How It Works

All you need to sign up is your name, e-mail and password.

From there, you will enter your phone number and custom messages to motivate yourself. And that's it! There is no price on mental health, so Safe Space will always be free to you.
