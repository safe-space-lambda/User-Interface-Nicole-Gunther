Our Mission

Safe Space is all about creating an environment that encourages self-love and self-motivation.

We believe that once you learn to love yourself, you'll have more inner stability, life becomes simpler, and you'll be happier.

“You yourself, as much as anybody in the entire universe, deserve your love and affection.” -Buddha

How It Works

The only things you need to get started are your name and a phone number eligible to receive texts. After signing up, you will be able to type custom messages for yourself that will be sent to you throughout the day.

And that's it!

If you are worried about the cost, no need! Mental health and self-love are very important to us, so Safe Space will always be free.
